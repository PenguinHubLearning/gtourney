G_tourney 2017

Where gaming meets pros

“I am a Gamer not because I don’t have a life, but because I choose to have MANY!” The event last year saw heavy participation from 1st year students. 19 teams battled each other in all thrilling 5 V/S 5 match of Counter-Strike 1.6. This year we will be back soon and guess what! All of you can be a part of this exciting gaming event. G_Tourney is the only independent event dedicated to gaming in IIT Roorkee. It is the growth of an idea that in today’s heavily connected world, we could come together, be creative, share experience and express ourselves in multitude of ways using video games. G_Tourney encourages people with all kinds of background to participate and contribute to this vast spread of gaming aura. Team BC++ was our winner for G_Tourney 2016 which was headed by Yuvraj Singh and was declared as the best team of Rajendra Bhawan 2015-16 batch. “Keep Calm & Let the Games Begin!”
